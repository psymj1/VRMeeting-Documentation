VRMeeting - Index

Introduction

VRMeeting is an Android VR experience with aim of allowing a presenter to host a meeting in which many participants will be able to listen in to what the presenter has to say.
The presenter has the opportunity to upload a PDf presentation which they can then navigate through which will also be displayed to the participants.
The project was sponsored by IBM and has been developed by the developers of Team Hexcore from the University of Nottingham
